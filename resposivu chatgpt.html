What is a wheelchair?

A wheelchair is a mobility device that allows people with mobility impairments to move around independently. Wheelchairs come in different types and designs to suit different needs and lifestyles.

What are the types of wheelchairs?

There are several types of wheelchairs, including:

  • Manual wheelchairs, which are propelled by the user's arms
  • Power wheelchairs, which are propelled by an electric motor
  • Sport wheelchairs, which are designed for sports and physical activities
  • Pediatric wheelchairs, which are designed for children

How do I choose a wheelchair?

Choosing a wheelchair depends on several factors, including:

  • Your level of mobility and strength
  • Your lifestyle and daily activities
  • Your body size and weight
  • Your budget and insurance coverage

It's important to consult with a healthcare professional or a wheelchair specialist to help you choose the right wheelchair for your needs.

How do I maintain my wheelchair?

Maintaining your wheelchair is important to ensure its safety and longevity. Here are some tips:

  • Clean your wheelchair regularly with a damp cloth
  • Check the tires and wheels for wear and tear
  • Inspect the brakes and locking mechanisms
  • Keep the battery charged if you have a power wheelchair

It's also a good idea to have your wheelchair serviced by a professional on a regular basis.
